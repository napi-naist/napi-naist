What is NAIST?

The Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST) is a school for graduate studies situated in the advanced technology research area of Osaka-Nara-Kyoto.


What is NAPI?

The NAIST Project for Interns (NAPI) offers yearly internship opportunities for students and faculty coming from the School of Science and Engineering (SOSE) of the Ateneo de Manila University (ADMU) in the Philippines.
