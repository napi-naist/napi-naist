Application Procedure

  1. Check if you are eligible to apply
  2. Choose a laboratory
  3. Accomplish the Recommender Information Form (Deadline: October 17, 2025)
  4. Accomplish and compile the Application Package and send to apps@napi-naist.jp (Deadline: October 24, 2025)
    • Application form
    • Curriculum vitae in specified format
    • Scanned copy of passport
    • Scanned copy of the certificate of enrollment or graduation
