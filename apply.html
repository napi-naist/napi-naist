  1. Check here if you are eligible to apply.
  2. Choose a laboratory here.
  3. Go through the application guidelines.
  4. Accomplish the recommender information form.
  5. Download and accomplish the following documents from the Application Kit:
    • Application form;
    • Curriculum vitae in specified format;
    • Scanned copy of passport; and
    • Scanned copy of the certificate of enrollment or graduation.
  6. Compile the documents.
  7. Send the application to napi@is.naist.jp by October 23, 2024 (11:59PM Philippine Standard Time).
