Application Procedure

Follow the steps below to formally apply to the NAPI program

  1. Check if you are eligible to apply
  2. Attend the NAPI Information Session on October 6, 2025 (Optional but recommended)
  3. Choose a laboratory
  4. Accomplish the Recommender Information Form (Deadline: October 17, 2025)
  5. Accomplish and compile the Application Package and upload to this link (Deadline: October 24, 2025)
    • Application form
    • Curriculum vitae in specified format
    • Scanned copy of passport
    • Scanned copy of certificate of enrollment or graduation


Handling of Personal Information

The collected documents, including the application form, CV, passport copy, and certificate of enrollment or graduation, will be used solely for the purposes of screening and processing applications for this program. All personal information will be handled with strict confidentiality and in accordance with relevant data protection regulations. Documents will not be shared with any third parties unrelated to the selection process and will be securely disposed of after use.
